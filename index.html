About Weather Passport

Weather Passport is your ultimate travel planning companion, providing comprehensive weather analysis to help you plan your trips with confidence. Developed with the latest Flutter technology, it offers a seamless experience across both iOS and Android platforms.

Features

  • Access to multiple weather data sources for accurate forecasts.
  • Historical weather analysis for informed travel planning.
  • Offline cache support for uninterrupted access.
  • Multi-language support including Turkish, English, Spanish, Russian, and Japanese.
  • Integration with Firebase for secure authentication and data management.

How It Works

Select your destination city and desired date range. Weather Passport fetches data from various APIs, processes it, and provides you with detailed weather insights. The app caches this data for offline access, ensuring you have the information you need, whenever you need it.
