About Weather Passport

Weather Passport is your ultimate travel planning companion, providing comprehensive weather analysis to help you plan your trips with confidence. Built with Flutter, it offers a beautiful, glassmorphic experience across your favorite devices.

✨ Join dozens of travelers who are already planning their adventures with Weather Passport!

Key Features

🌍 Multiple Data Sources

Access accurate forecasts from OpenMeteo and VisualCrossing.

⏳ Historical Analysis

Analyze city-based historical weather to plan your trips better.

🧳 Suitcase Preparation

Interactive packing checklist based on trip temperature, synced to Firestore in real-time.

✨ Glassmorphic UI

Redesigned Favorites Screen with live packing progress and color-coded temperature details.

⚡ Quick Refresh

One-tap query for current forecasts and direct Firestore stats updates from favorite cards.

🔗 Deep Linking

Quick app navigation via custom URL schemes (weatherpassport://).

📶 Offline First

Support for offline caching with bypass flags for uninterrupted access.

🌐 Global Ready

Multi-language support: Turkish, English, Spanish, Russian, and Japanese.

🔐 Secure & Cloud

Firebase Auth, Apple/Google Sign-in, and real-time Firestore synchronization.

💎 Premium Features

Enhanced experience with RevenueCat integration for premium subscriptions.
