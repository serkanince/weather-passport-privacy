Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in case of an error in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data. We use industry-standard security measures to safeguard your information.

Account Deletion

Users can permanently delete their account information through the following steps:

  1. Go to the in-app Settings menu
  2. Select "Support & Feedback" section
  3. Click "Delete Account" option

The account deletion process:

  • Permanently removes all your personal data
  • Is an irreversible action
  • Does not require additional customer service approval
  • Includes a final confirmation step to prevent accidental deletion
